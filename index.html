About

Find Out More About Us

  • Welcome to Hostel-mess management website! It is the free to use website created by students of PICT which provides unrestricted access to all the hostels and mess available in the vicinity of PICT. This website also provides a free and open source platform to hostel and mess owners to showcase their hostels to a large group of students. With the help of our website, students can save the charges for broker/middleman which they have to pay while searching hostel offline.

  • This website not only provides the hostel/mess information but also comes up with the comparison between them so the students can choose the best one according to their necessity. We will regularly update the website and extend the scope so that the maximum students will be benefitted. Your support will drive us to do our best. We appreciate you for visiting our website.
